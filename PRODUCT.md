# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Pluto serves dance music fans, event attendees, artists, volunteers, and crew. During ManaFest, people may be outdoors, moving between stages, checking information quickly on a phone, and dealing with inconsistent connectivity or low light.

## Product Purpose

Pluto is the home for private underground events and the attendee experience around them. It should make event discovery, festival guidance, schedules, updates, rewards, and account tools feel cohesive and dependable before and during an event.

## Brand Personality

Underground, expressive, and welcoming. The interface should feel connected to the artwork and energy of the events while remaining calm enough to use when someone needs an answer quickly.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic corporate event dashboards, polished luxury-festival styling, neon-on-black crypto aesthetics, and decorative layouts that bury practical information. Do not make operational pages feel like marketing pages.

## Design Principles

1. Put the day-of answer first.
2. Carry the event artwork into the interface without competing with the content.
3. Use strong hierarchy and familiar controls so the app works at a glance.
4. Let personality appear in focused moments, not every surface.
5. Design mobile-first for outdoor, low-attention use while keeping desktop composed.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain strong text contrast, clear focus and selected states, comfortable touch targets, readable body sizes, and layouts that remain usable with text scaling and reduced motion. Never rely on color alone to communicate status or urgency.
